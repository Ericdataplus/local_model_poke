POKEMON CRYSTAL WALKTHROUGH

SECTION 1: NEW BARK TOWN
1. You start in your room (Player's House 2F).
2. Go to the PC in the corner if you want to withdraw a Potion (optional).
3. Go downstairs to Player's House 1F.
4. Talk to your Mom. She will give you the PokeGear.
5. Exit the house to New Bark Town.
6. Go to Elm's Lab (the large building next to your house).
7. Talk to Professor Elm. He will offer you a Pokemon.
8. Choose your starter Pokemon:
   - Cyndaquil (Fire)
   - Totodile (Water)
   - Chikorita (Grass)
9. After choosing, Elm will give you his phone number.
10. Try to leave the lab. The aide will give you a Potion.
11. Exit the lab and head West (Left) to Route 29.

SECTION 2: ROUTE 29
1. Travel West through the route.
2. You cannot catch Pokemon yet (no Pokeballs).
3. Avoid tall grass if you want to save health, or fight to gain XP.
4. Continue West until you reach Cherrygrove City.

SECTION 3: CHERRYGROVE CITY
1. As you enter, an old man will give you a tour. Follow him.
2. He will give you the Map Card for your PokeGear.
3. Heal at the Pokemon Center (Red roof building).
4. Head North (Up) to Route 30.

SECTION 4: ROUTE 30 & MR. POKEMON'S HOUSE
1. Go North through Route 30.
2. You will see a house with a Berry tree.
3. Continue North until you reach Mr. Pokemon's House.
4. Enter the house and talk to Mr. Pokemon. He gives you the Mystery Egg.
5. Professor Oak is there too. He gives you the Pokedex.
6. Exit the house. Professor Elm calls you. Something bad happened at the lab!
7. Return to Cherrygrove City.

SECTION 5: RIVAL BATTLE
1. As you try to leave Cherrygrove City (heading East back to New Bark), you will be stopped by a red-haired boy (Rival).
2. Battle him!
   - If you picked Cyndaquil, he has Totodile.
   - If you picked Totodile, he has Chikorita.
   - If you picked Chikorita, he has Cyndaquil.
3. Use your damaging moves (Tackle/Scratch). Use Potion if HP is low.
4. After winning, he runs away.

SECTION 6: RETURN TO NEW BARK TOWN
1. Go East through Route 29 back to New Bark Town.
2. Enter Elm's Lab.
3. A policeman is there. Name your rival (Silver is the canon name).
4. Give the Mystery Egg to Elm.
5. Talk to the aide to get 5 Pokeballs.
6. Now you can catch Pokemon!
7. Leave the lab. Mom will ask if you want to save money. (Your choice).
8. Head West to Route 29 again.

SECTION 7: JOURNEY TO VIOLET CITY
1. Go through Route 29 to Cherrygrove City.
2. Go North to Route 30.
3. This time, the path North is open (trainers were blocking it before).
4. Battle trainers on Route 30 to level up.
5. Continue North to Route 31.
6. You can catch Bellsprout, Pidgey, Rattata, Hoothoot (Night), Spinarak (Night), Ledyba (Morning).
7. On Route 31, there is a cave (Dark Cave). You can explore the entrance but need Flash to see.
8. Continue West to Violet City.

SECTION 8: VIOLET CITY
1. Heal at the Pokemon Center.
2. Go to Sprout Tower (North part of town).
   - Battle Sages (they use Bellsprout).
   - Get HM05 (Flash) from the Elder at the top.
   - Escape Rope out or walk down.
3. Go to the Violet City Gym (Falkner).
   - Type: Flying.
   - Strategy: Use Rock or Electric moves if you have them (Geodude can be caught on Route 46 or Dark Cave). Or just over-level your starter.
   - Defeat Falkner to get the Zephyr Badge.
4. Professor Elm calls you. The Egg is at the PokeCenter.
5. Go to the PokeCenter and talk to the aide to get the Egg (Togepi).
6. Head South to Route 32.
